Work

The Equinox project developed multiple app pages for the EQX app. The pages were revolved around gym goers interactions with checking in and tracking progress. This project was a three stage process: research - ideation - prototype.

We focused on developing multiple views for tracking progress in gym attendance. We also tracked progress through data metrics of certain exercises.

The Marketwake project followed the complete desing of a client facing dashboard for a strategy marketing company.

Each component within the dashboard is interactive and can be clicked into. We conducted research to determine which components on the dashboard are most fitting to the user.

U4ea is a startup in which I desinged a mobile and website wirefram for. The wireframe was for their merchandise website.

Each wireframe is designed with multiple pages and was purposely set to adhere to current fashion website trends.
